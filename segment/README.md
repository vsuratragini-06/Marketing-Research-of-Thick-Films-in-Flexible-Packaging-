# Segments

## Overview

Thick films are high-performance flexible packaging films designed to provide enhanced strength, durability, puncture resistance, and product protection compared to conventional packaging films. Typically used in applications requiring higher load-bearing capacity and superior barrier performance, thick films play a critical role in food, agriculture, industrial, chemical, healthcare, and consumer goods packaging.

---

## Key Characteristics

- High tensile and puncture strength
- Superior seal integrity
- Enhanced moisture and oxygen barrier properties
- Improved durability during transportation
- Suitable for heavy-duty and bulk packaging applications
- Available in mono-layer and multi-layer structures

---

| Category | Thickness |

## Industry Importance

The growing demand for lightweight, durable, and sustainable packaging solutions has positioned thick films as a key component of the global flexible packaging industry. Advancements in recyclable mono-material structures such as BOPE and MDO-PE are further driving innovation and future growth within this segment.
